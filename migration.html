COVID-19 Response

Work Restrictions waived for International Nursing Students

Australian Prime Minister, Scott Morrison, announced drastic measures to overcome healthcare staff shortage in hospitals.

Work restrictions would be lifted on some 20,000 international nursing students currently studying in Australia. They are going to be available to support the health effort right across the country, as directed by Australian Health Officials. Australia is not importing nurses into the country. This technically means that they will have unlimited work rights instead of the 40 hour fortnightly work limitation.

Visitor Visa

DO NOT OVERSTAY YOUR VISA

If you are in Australia on a visitor visa, your visa allows you to be in Australia for 3, 6 or 12 months

Please ensure that you do not overstay your visa. There are systems in place for visitors onshore to apply for a further visa, to make their status lawful.

This is very important if their visa has a no further stay condition 8503 on their visa grant.

This condition precludes those visa holders applying for a further visa while they are onshore, except for certain prescribed visa categories. It is advisable to obtain appropriate immigration advise to resolve their visa status.
